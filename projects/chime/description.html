Chime is a 3D platformer where you play as a mechanical bird that must find its way home at the top of a grandfather clock.

Notes:

  • Development time: 6 months
  • Group size: 7
  • Date: October 2025 - March 2026

What I Did:

  • Created environmental interactions in C++
  • Created a direct solve constraint-based gear system
  • Designed systems and tools in C++ to allow level designers to create puzzles using blueprints

Tools Used

Created in Unreal Engine 5.
